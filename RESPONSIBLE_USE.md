# Responsible Use Guidelines

## ⚠️ Important Notice

WatserFace is a derivative of FaceFusion with **modified safety features**:

### Removed Safeguards
1. **NSFW Content Detection**: Disabled in version 0.10.0
2. **Tamper Validation**: Bypassed to enable development and customization

### User Responsibilities

By using WatserFace, you agree to:

1. **Legal Compliance**: Ensure your use complies with local laws and regulations
2. **Consent**: Only process images/videos with proper authorization from all parties
3. **No Harmful Use**: Do not create content for:
   - Non-consensual deepfakes or intimate imagery
   - Identity theft or fraud
   - Misinformation or disinformation campaigns
   - Harassment, defamation, or abuse
4. **Attribution**: Credit subjects and disclose AI manipulation when sharing results
5. **Disclosure**: Clearly mark AI-generated or AI-modified content

### Authorized Use Cases

✅ **Permitted**:
- Academic research with institutional review board (IRB) approval
- Film/VFX production with actor consent and contracts
- Personal creative projects for non-commercial use
- Security research and penetration testing
- Educational demonstrations in controlled environments
- Artistic expression with proper subject consent

❌ **Prohibited**:
- Creating non-consensual intimate imagery (deepfake pornography)
- Impersonation for fraud, phishing, or financial crimes
- Political misinformation or election interference
- Defamation, harassment, or targeted abuse
- Circumventing biometric authentication systems
- Creating misleading evidence for legal proceedings

### Ethical Guidelines

**Transparency**: Always disclose when content has been AI-manipulated.

**Consent**: Obtain explicit permission before using someone's likeness.

**Context**: Consider the social and political implications of your work.

**Accountability**: Take responsibility for how your creations are used.

**Reversibility**: Where possible, provide ways to verify or detect manipulations.
